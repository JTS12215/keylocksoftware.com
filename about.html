The KeyLock Mission

In an era where personal data is treated as a commodity, KeyLock was born from a simple necessity: Absolute Sovereignty.

Why Local-First?

Cloud-based password managers are centralized "honeypots." If their servers are breached, millions of identities are at risk. KeyLock flips the script. By keeping your encrypted vault on your own hardware, you eliminate the middleman and the cloud-risk entirely.

Security Without Compromise

KeyLock uses industrial-grade Fernet (AES) encryption. We don't have your keys, we don't have your data, and we don't have a "backdoor." You are the sole administrator of your digital life.
