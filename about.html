The KeyLock Mission

In an era where personal data is treated as a commodity, KeyLock was born from a simple necessity: Absolute Data Sovereignty.

Why Local-First?

KeyLock was born from a simple idea: Your data should never be a target. By removing the centralized commercial cloud, we remove the incentive for hackers. Your vault is your own. Whether you keep it on a local machine, thumb drive, Google or Dropbox, you are the only one with the key.

Transparent & Open Source

KeyLock is free to use, redistribute and open for inspection. You can review our source code on GitHub or get started today by visiting our Docs page and the Getting Started section of the manual.

About KeyLock

We believe that security shouldn't be a subscription service or a cloud-based risk. KeyLock was built for those who demand absolute control over their data.
